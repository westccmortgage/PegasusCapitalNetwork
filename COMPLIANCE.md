# Pegasus Network — Compliance Statement

Pegasus Lenders Group LLC operates **Pegasus Network** as a **membership marketplace platform**.

**Pegasus is NOT:**
- a mortgage lender
- a mortgage loan originator (MLO)
- a broker-dealer
- a real estate broker
- a registered investment advisor (RIA)

**Operating principles:**
- All connections made through the platform are **member-directed**. Pegasus does not originate, underwrite, fund, or broker loans, and does not provide investment, legal, or tax advice.
- Scores produced by the Pegasus Match Engine (Capital Alignment, Deal Strength, Funding Probability, Documentation Readiness, Risk Level) are **informational signals only** — not commitments, approvals, or recommendations to lend or borrow.
- Users **must consult licensed professionals** for all financing, legal, tax, and investment decisions.

This language appears in the global footer (`pegasus-core.js → Pegasus.footer()`) and must remain on every page.
