Why it matters

Small Changes Can Move the Review Path

The same purchase can land on a different review path depending on a few inputs. These are educational illustrations — not approvals or eligibility decisions.

  • A different county can change the reference line, since limits are set county by county.
  • A larger down payment can move the estimated loan closer to — or below — the county line.
  • Taxes, insurance, flood, HOA, and mortgage insurance can change the real estimated monthly picture.
  • Estimated rent can affect DSCR coverage on an investment scenario.
  • Buydown math depends on the cost, the estimated monthly savings, and how long you expect to hold the loan.
  • Occupancy and property type may change which review path should be considered.
