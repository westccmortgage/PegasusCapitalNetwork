Business pages
Business page = promotion

Promote what you represent

A business page is a premium, mini-website for a company, project, fund, or capital program. It says what you do, what you offer, and who should connect — and it’s tied to your trusted profile.

  • One page per company, project, fund, or program
  • What you do · what you offer · who should connect
  • Hosts your opportunities and showcases

Opportunities & showcases
Opportunity / showcase = presentation

Present something specific

Opportunities and showcases are individual presentations — a capital program, project, listing, startup need, showcase of completed work, event, or partnership request. Each format looks distinct, and showcases lead with visuals.

  • Seven formats, each with its own visual treatment
  • Showcases are visual-first: cover, gallery, video, story
  • Capital programs are information-first: who it helps, size, market

Share Studio
Share Studio = distribution

Bring people back to Pegasus

Share Studio turns any profile, business page, or presentation into share-ready assets — platform previews, a ready caption, a clean link, and download-ready images for LinkedIn, Facebook, X, and Instagram. You confirm every post.

  • Previews for LinkedIn / Facebook / X, Instagram post & story
  • Editable caption, copy link, download image
  • Instagram-ready images — never auto-posted on your behalf

05
Requests
Requests = connection

The right people reach out

When someone wants to connect or expresses interest, it arrives as a request — with a reason and a short message. Curated, never cold. You decide who to engage; private contact details are never exposed.

  • Connection requests carry an intent and a note
  • Recipients are notified in their bell and Workspace
  • No emails or phone numbers exposed
06
Workspace
Workspace = private work

Where the work happens

Your Workspace is the private side of Pegasus — requests, conversations, deal rooms, CRM, and follow-up. It’s not the front door; the Members Network is your home. Workspace is where momentum is managed.

  • Triage requests and accepted interest
  • Deal rooms, CRM, and private follow-up
  • Private by default — your operations, not a public feed
